The Preservation Standard

Modern vehicle wiring is increasingly wrapped in soy-based biodegradable insulation. While environmentally sound, this insulation acts as a direct food source attraction for rodents, nesting inside warm engine blocks during humid monsoons and winter drafts.

Mr. Mech solves this through a non-sticky, non-invasive active scent shield. By depositing an intense scent barrier, it repels pests instantly without the need for toxic domestic poisons or sticky chemical residues that attract dirt and degrade electrical sleeves.

Formulation Features

Dual-Action Thermal Scent Shield Blends active peppermint and eucalyptus oils to target and over-stimulate rodent olfactory glands, instantly warding off pest intrusions.
240°C Heat Stability Engineered to bind securely to composite surfaces and rubber wire sheaths, remaining completely active under high engine running temperatures.
100% Surface Compatibility Absolutely safe on natural rubber hoses, PVC wiring sleeves, aluminum engine parts, and fiberglass panels.

motorsport Drivechain Maintenance

Motorcycle chains rotate at extreme speeds under high friction, fully exposed to Indian road environments. Fine sand, monsoonal mud, and sticky lubes bind together to form an abrasive grinding paste that rapidly eats sprocket teeth and rollers.

Standard degreasers are often too aggressive, penetrating and degrading the delicate rubber O-rings that seal grease inside chain rollers. Mr. Mech Chain Cleaner strips grit, monsoonal mud, and oxidized wax instantly while keeping seals completely intact.

Formulation Features

High-Pressure Jet Delivery An aggressive, fast-vaporizing spray that physically blasts loose contaminants from sprockets and link plates.
O-Ring & X-Ring Rubber Safety Formulated specifically with non-chlorinated hydrocarbon solvents that do not swell, crack, or dry out delicate rubber rollers.
Zero Post-Drying Residue Dries completely clean within 3 minutes, leaving raw metal ready to bond perfectly with fresh clean chain lubricant.
